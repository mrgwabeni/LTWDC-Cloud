SALES MANAGEMENT

ONLINE SHORT COURSE

Gain the skills needed to structure, train, and lead a sales team in order to make an impact in your business. 9 weeks, excluding orientation. 8–10 hours of self-paced learning per week, entirely online. REGISTER NOW Email: uctonline@getsmarter.com Call: +27 87 551 8063

ABOUT THIS COURSE

Successful sales managers need to master compelling sales techniques and leadership skills to lead the way with a competitive strategy. On the course, students will learn to implement management best practices to benefit their business. From forecasting and budgets to recruitment and performance evaluation, this University of Cape Town (UCT) Sales Management online short course covers a broad range of skills designed to equip effective sales managers. ​ “I learned a lot of methods that I have always doubted, and now I am more confident in what I am executing in my sales career. It also boosts confidence when networking with the classmates and realising your topic has some valid points and useful aspects to others. It gives you a clear insight into what is happening in the industry, with up-to-date communication and articles I never thought I'd be exposed to.” Monique Bekker Newly Appointed Sales Manager

WHAT THIS COURSE COVERS

During this nine-week online short course, you’ll identify the context and principles that underpin the customer focus in sales and marketing. Guided by UCT faculty, you’ll outline personal selling best practices for different circumstances, and show how each stage contributes to closing a sale and strengthening customer relationships. By the end of the course, you’ll have developed the skills to design a training programme, justify the need for integrating technology and CRM systems in sales management planning, and assess performance from a business perspective.

WHAT YOU’LL LEARN

ORIENTATION MODULE Welcome to Your Online Campus MODULE 1 Sales and Marketing in the 21st Century MODULE 2 The Personal Selling Function MODULE 3 Stages in the Personal Selling Process MODULE 4 Sales Forecasting, Budgeting, and Pricing Strategies MODULE 5 Sales Planning and Recruitment MODULE 6 Sales Training and Developing MODULE 7 The Role of Technology in Sales and Sales Management MODULE 8 Leadership, Management, and Compensating the Sales Team MODULE 9 Analysis and Evaluation of Sales Team Performance and Customer Management

WHO SHOULD TAKE THIS COURSE

Those currently working in sales, and those aspiring to advance or validate their managerial and strategic capabilities, would benefit from participating in this course. It’s also suited to those wanting to transition into a role in sales, as it equips students with a comprehensive set of management-level sales techniques, processes, and strategies. Past students have had titles within business development, customer service, sales and marketing, account management, and come from a variety of industries. THIS COURSE IS FOR YOU IF YOU WANT TO:

ADVANCE YOUR SKILLS

Advance your sales skills by learning best practices in modern sales techniques, strategies, and leadership to ensure you’re performing your sales role to the best of your ability.

DEVELOP NEW COMPETENCIES

Gain confidence in your ability to effectively lead and direct the sales function within any organisation.

IMPROVE SALES PROCESSES

Learn practical skills in sales management, such as how to select forecasting methods, create a sales budget, build a sales team, choose appropriate CRM systems, perform cost analysis, and identify pricing strategies.

ABOUT THE CERTIFICATE

Earn a certificate from UCT, Africa’s leading university, as recognition of your newly developed skills. Assessment is continuous and based on a series of practical assignments completed online. In order to be issued with a University of Cape Town certificate of completion you’ll need to meet the requirements outlined in the course handbook. The handbook will be made available to you as soon as you begin the course. Your certificate will be issued in your legal name and sent to you upon successful completion of the course, as per the stipulated requirements. WHO YOU’LL LEARN FROM This subject matter expert from UCT guides the course design and appears in a number of course videos, along with a variety of industry professionals.
